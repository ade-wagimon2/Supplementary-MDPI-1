# ⚛️ Protein-Ligand Interaction Fingerprint Analysis (ProLIF)

This directory contains the computational pipeline and visualization scripts used to extract, compare, and plot protein-ligand interaction fingerprints for the study: **"Comparative Structural Modeling Suggests Distinct Signatures of Conformational Plasticity and Surface Physicochemistry in Phytoene Synthase and Dehydrosqualene Synthase."**

## 📖 Overview

To understand the distinct binding modes and substrate specificities of Phytoene Synthase (PSY) and Dehydrosqualene Synthase (CrtM), we performed rigorous Protein-Ligand Interaction Fingerprint (PLIF) analysis. Using **ProLIF** (Protein-Ligand Interaction Fingerprints) integrated with **MDAnalysis**, we quantified the frequency of specific non-covalent interactions (e.g., Hydrophobic, H-Bonds, Anionic, VdW contacts) across multiple independent MD replicas. 

The pipeline is split into two phases:
1. **Extraction & Categorization** (`Full_interactions.ipynb`): Processes raw MD trajectories to calculate interaction frequencies and categorizes them into common vs. ligand-specific interactions.
2. **Publication-Ready Visualization** (`interaction_analysis_restyle.py`): Consumes the extracted CSV data to generate high-resolution, Nature-style comparative figures.

---

## 🧬 Systems Analyzed

The analysis compares the binding profiles of two distinct ligands across two different enzymes:

| Enzyme | Ligand 1 (GGPP analog) | Ligand 2 (FSPP analog) | Cofactor |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PSY** (Phytoene Synthase) | `UN*` | `LG*` | `MG` (Mg2+) |
| **CrtM** (Dehydrosqualene Synthase) | `lg*` | `IG*` | `MG` (Mg2+) |

*Note: Ligand residue names (`resnames`) may vary slightly depending on the specific PDB/AMBER parameterization used in the input topologies.*

## 🗂️ File Descriptions

### 📓 `Full_interactions.ipynb`
The core Jupyter Notebook that executes the heavy computational lifting.
* **Loads** AMBER topologies (`.prmtop`) and NetCDF trajectories (`.nc`).
* **Selects** protein residues within a `10.0 Å` distance cutoff of the main ligand.
* **Runs** ProLIF to calculate interactions every `50th` frame.
* **Normalizes** MultiIndex dataframes to group interactions by `(Protein_Residue, Interaction_Type)`.
* **Categorizes** interactions into:
  * **Common**: >50% frequency in both ligand systems.
  * **Unique**: >50% in one ligand, but ≤50% in the other.
* **Outputs** raw CSVs, heatmaps, scatter plots, and ProLIF barcode plots.

### 🐍 `interaction_analysis_restyle.py`
A Python script dedicated to generating publication-quality (Nature-style) figures from the CSVs generated by the notebook.
* **`plot_fingerprint()`**: Generates grouped bar plots comparing FSPP vs GGPP frequencies for a single system.
* **`plot_interaction_panel()`**: Creates a 3-panel vertical figure (Common, Unique to GGPP, Unique to FSPP).
* **`plot_combined_interaction_panel()`**: Generates a comprehensive 3x2 grid comparing PSY and CrtM side-by-side, complete with A/B/C panel labels, optimized spacing, and professional color palettes.
